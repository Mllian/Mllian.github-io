NotReal Menu

All of the food not served is completely vegan!




NotReal Vegan Burgers

Our NotReal Burgers use 100% vegan patties and cheese, with a wide selection of extras and sauces. Fries are included and gluten free options are available!



NotReal Vegan Pizzas

NotReal Vegan Pizzas use a tradtional thin, stonebaked base with a range of toppings. Our vegan cheese is so good you wont know the difference and all toppings can be customised to your own preference. Gluten free bases and toppings available!



NotReal Salads

Looking for something a little lighter? Our Salads are anything but boring and contain as much flavour as they do goodness. Why not try our house croutons or top with This Isn't Bacon pieces?*

NotReal Vegan Desserts

Room for more? Take a look at our selection of completely vegan desserts. A firm favorite is our cinammon rolls, baked fresh in house every day!
